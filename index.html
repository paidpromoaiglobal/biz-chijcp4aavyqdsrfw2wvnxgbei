About Us

T CUBE Physiotherapy & Sports Rehab Clinic

T CUBE Physiotherapy & Sports Rehab Clinic is a trusted healthcare destination located in Ganapathy Maanagar, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Founded with a passion for restoring movement and enhancing athletic performance, our clinic combines evidence-based physiotherapy techniques with personalized care plans to help patients recover from injuries, manage chronic pain, and return to their active lifestyles. Our highly trained therapists bring dedication and expertise to every session, earning us a perfect 5-star rating from our valued patients.

Whether you are an athlete looking to bounce back from a sports injury or an individual seeking relief from everyday musculoskeletal discomfort, T CUBE is here for you. Conveniently situated near the Bannari Amman Kovil in the 4th Block, we offer flexible hours Monday through Saturday — including round-the-clock availability — ensuring that expert care is never out of reach. What We Offer

Our Services

Professional care tailored to your needs

🏥

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs tailored for athletes recovering from sprains, strains, ligament tears, and other sports-related injuries to ensure a safe and speedy return to sport.

💊

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy

Targeted treatment for joint pain, back pain, neck pain, and muscle disorders using manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and advanced modalities.

🩺

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured recovery plans following orthopedic surgeries such as ACL repair, knee replacement, or shoulder surgery to restore strength, mobility, and function.

📋

Electrotherapy & Modalities

Advanced therapeutic techniques including ultrasound therapy, TENS, and IFT to accelerate tissue healing, reduce inflammation, and relieve chronic pain.

💡

Neurological Physiotherapy

Specialized care for patients with neurological conditions such as stroke, Parkinson's disease, or nerve injuries, focusing on improving balance, coordination, and daily function.

⚕️

Posture Correction & Ergonomic Training

Assessment and correction of postural imbalances caused by sedentary lifestyles, prolonged desk work, or repetitive movements to prevent future injuries.

✦

Pediatric & Geriatric Physiotherapy

Gentle and effective physiotherapy programs designed specifically for children with developmental challenges and elderly patients managing age-related mobility issues.

✦

Home Visit Physiotherapy

Convenient in-home physiotherapy sessions for patients who are unable to travel to the clinic, ensuring uninterrupted care and recovery in a comfortable environment.
